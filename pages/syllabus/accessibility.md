# Accessibility

Students requesting accommodations due to a disability must provide a current Authorization for Accommodation (AFA) letter, issued by the Office for Students with Disabilities (OSD) in University Center 202. To arrange accommodations, contact Kyle ([kshannon@ucsd.edu](mailto:kshannon@ucsd.edu)) privately and he will loop in the appropriate course staff.

The OSD can help you further:
- 858.534.4382
- [osd@ucsd.edu](mailto:osd@ucsd.edu)
- [disabilities.ucsd.edu](http://disabilities.ucsd.edu)
