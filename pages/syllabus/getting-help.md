# Getting Help

TAs and IAs answer questions during normal working hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm). Reach out whenever works for you, but you may not hear back until the next day, so don't wait until the last minute. For a true emergency, email Kyle with **EMERGENCY-COGS9** in the subject line.

Where to take your question:
- **Course content:** post it so everyone benefits, or ask during office hours.
- **Course logistics:** check the syllabus first; if it's not there, ask in the Project Studio, a classmate, or office hours.
- **Something cool to share, or an in-depth chat:** email Kyle ([kshannon@ucsd.edu](mailto:kshannon@ucsd.edu)) or come to office hours. Put **COGS9** in the subject line.
