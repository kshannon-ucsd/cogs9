# Conduct & Community

In all interactions in this class, be respectful. This includes following the UC San Diego [principles of the community](https://ucsd.edu/about/principles.html).

This class is a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or political beliefs. Always refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech. Take care of each other.

If you have a concern, please speak with Kyle or your TA. If you are uncomfortable doing so, the [OPHD](https://blink.ucsd.edu/HR/policies/sexual/OPHD.html) (Office for the Prevention of Sexual Harassment and Discrimination) and [CARE](https://care.ucsd.edu/) (confidential advocacy for sexual and gender-based violence) are great resources on campus.
