# Project Proposal

The proposal locks in your group's data science question and dataset before you start Part 1. It is **required but not graded**; you just need to turn it in on time.

Once your group is formed (via the [time-zone sign-up sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1mPOM9M8D4A-gajRI4acaubvKKn60JWY4xTXnq0A1J14/edit?usp=sharing)), submit your proposal with this form:

### [Project Proposal form](https://forms.gle/eDpE4M1nsEc1xve18)

**One submission per group.**

## What the form asks for

- All team members' info.
- Your project **title**.
- The **dataset** you plan to use (with a link).
- A **short description** of your project.

## Details

- **Required, not graded**, but you still must submit it on time.
- **Due:** Friday of Week 2, 11:59 pm.
- **One submission per group** (not per person).

Not sure where to find data? See the [Example Datasets]({{ '/final-group-project/example-datasets' | relative_url }}) page.
