# Joining the Class Late

Summer sessions move fast. If you added COGS 9 after it started, work through this checklist **right away** so you do not fall behind. None of it takes long.

{: .important }
Want full credit for Week 1 work? Let the course staff know on [Piazza]({{ course_vars.piazza_link }}) **before the end of Week 2**, so we do not count your early work as late. See the [late policy]({{ '/syllabus#grading' | relative_url }}) for details.

## Your catch-up checklist

1. **Get on Piazza.** Announcements, questions, and answers all happen on [Piazza]({{ course_vars.piazza_link }}). Make sure you can see the class and turn on notifications. It is the fastest way to reach staff and classmates.

2. **Check you are on Gradescope.** Every quiz and assignment is submitted on [Gradescope]({{ course_vars.gradescope_link }}). You are auto-enrolled, but if you do not see the course, post on [Piazza]({{ course_vars.piazza_link }}) or email your TA, and we will add you.

3. **Find a project group.** The final project is done in groups of 3-4, grouped by time zone.
    - Add your name, email, and time zone to the [group sign-up sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1mPOM9M8D4A-gajRI4acaubvKKn60JWY4xTXnq0A1J14/edit?usp=sharing).
    - Email your TA to help place you. Include your **name**, **email**, **time zone**, and whether you already have teammates.
    - See the [Project Studio]({{ '/final-group-project/group-work-primer' | relative_url }}) page for how groups work.

4. **Start the first assignment and readings now.** These come due first, so do not wait:
    - [Assignment 1]({{ '/assignment/assignment-1' | relative_url }})
    - [Reading 1]({{ '/readings/reading-1' | relative_url }}) and [Reading 2]({{ '/readings/reading-2' | relative_url }}), then take Reading Quizzes 1 and 2 on [Gradescope]({{ course_vars.gradescope_link }}).

Everything you need (due dates, links, lectures) is on this website. When in doubt, check the [home page]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) calendar or ask on Piazza.
